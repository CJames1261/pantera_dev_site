Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

Pantera Claw ("we," "our," or "us") operates www.agenticaiutah.com. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We designed this site to collect as little personal data as possible.

Information we collect

When you submit our contact form we receive the name, email address, optional company name, and message you provide. We use this only to reply to your inquiry and, if it leads to a project, to onboard you as a client.

We use Vercel to host the site, which logs IP address, user agent, and basic request metadata for security and performance monitoring. We do not run any third-party analytics or advertising trackers on this site.

How we use your information

  • To respond to questions you send through the contact form.
  • To deliver and improve our consulting services if you become a client.
  • To meet legal, accounting, and security obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Cookies

This site does not set marketing or analytics cookies. Vercel may set minimal infrastructure cookies for security and load balancing.

Client engagements

When we work on a paid engagement we typically receive access to client systems and data. We treat that information as confidential under the terms of the engagement contract. We do not use client data to train third-party AI models, and we do not share it outside our team without written permission.

Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you. Email info@panteraclaw.com and we will respond within 30 days.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our practices change or to reflect new legal requirements. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
