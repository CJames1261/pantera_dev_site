How we work

A four-step engagement, not a black box

  1. 01

    Discovery sprint

    A 1–2 week paid engagement where we map your existing data, identify the highest-leverage problem, and produce a written scope and fixed-price proposal. No commitment to continue.

  2. 02

    Build sprints

    Two-week iterations with a working demo at the end of each. You see real progress on your real data, not a Gantt chart. You can adjust scope between sprints.

  3. 03

    Calibration

    We run the deliverable against actual users and real workloads for two weeks. We tune, fix, and retrain until it holds up under daily use.

  4. 04

    Handover

    Your team receives the source code, infrastructure, and runbooks, plus a 30-day stabilization window included at no additional cost. Optional retainer afterward.
